COMIC BOOK PRESSING & CLEANING IS NOW LIVE

Fantasy Road is pleased to announce the launch of our pressing & cleaning services!

Do you have a favourite book that's slowly been accumulating dust? A treasured issue that just sits there, slowly losing its value? Or are you a comic book trader? An enthusiast that has their eye on a certain book with high speculation value? Well look no further.

Pressing & cleaning is the must-do first step when you start your book's journey along the Fantasy Road to CGC grading. Why? Well, CGC graders use advanced techniques to determine your books value and grade, and this includes identifiying things such as thread strains, fingerprints and the like that is not visible to the naked eye. Pressing & cleaning your books before CGC submission gives your issues their very best fighting chance at that perfect grade.

Fantasy Road also specialises in bumping the grade of prior-graded books, by carefully cracking the CGC protective case and removing from the inner-layer, cleaning, pressing and resubmitting.

Every book on the Fantasy Road is eligible for cleaning & pressing services, just check the "pressing & cleaning" box on the relevant issue at checkout. To submit your own books to us, check out the services page for a run-down of the process, then submit the comic book services submission form there, or below.

CGC SUBMISSION MIDDLEMAN SERVICE IS NOW LIVE!

Fantasy Road is pleased to announce the launch of our CGC Submission Middleman services!

So... You've just submitted that precious book of yours for cleaning and pressing, giving your beloved issue the best possible chance it can to achieve its highest grade. But wait! This is a speculation book, set to a specific timescale that will drastically affect its value! Perhaps the episode it's tied in with has aired, or the trailer for the big movie has just dropped, and time is of the essence... Never fear! CGC Submission is here.

Acting as a middleman between you and the Registered CGC trader, Fantasy Road can cut your submission time by over half. No longer will you need to wait for the books to arrive back to you, open them, go to the registered CGC trader's website, check out their submission guidelines, print off the form, fulfil the submission requirements, repackage the books, wrap them up, take them to the post office and resubmit them. All you have to do is select the Fantasy Road CGC submission middleman service at checkout, or on your pressing & cleaning submission form, and we'll handle the boring bits for you.
