COMIC BOOK PRESSING & CLEANING IS NOW LIVE

Fantasy Road is pleased to announce the launch of our pressing & cleaning services!

Do you have a favourite book that's slowly been accumulating dust? A treasured issue that just sits there, slowly losing its value? Or are you a comic book trader? An enthusiast that has their eye on a certain book with high speculation value? Well look no further.

Pressing & cleaning is the must-do first step when you start your book's journey along the Fantasy Road to CGC grading. Why? Well, CGC graders use advanced techniques to determine your books value and grade, and this includes identifiying things such as thread strains, fingerprints and the like that is not visible to the naked eye. Pressing & cleaning your books before CGC submission gives your issues their very best fighting chance at that perfect grade.

Fantasy Road also specialises in bumping the grade of prior-graded books, by carefully cracking the CGC protective case and removing from the inner-layer, cleaning, pressing and resubmitting.

Every book on the Fantasy Road is eligible for cleaning & pressing services, just check the "pressing & cleaning" box on the relevant issue at checkout. To submit your own books to us, check out the services page for a run-down of the process, then submit the comic book services submission form there, or below.

Fantasy Road is pleased to announce the launch of our CGC Submission Middleman services!

So... You've just submitted that precious book of yours for cleaning and pressing, giving your beloved issue the best possible chance it can to achieve its highest grade. But wait! RECYCLING ON THE FANTASY ROAD

One of the thing's that we've never quite been able to understand, not just limited to comic book stores, but online and postal orders in general, is the sheer volume of enviromental waste that the retail industry produces.

I'm sure, that as a comic book collector, or as a person that knows a comic book collector, you have at one point in your life at least noticed, if not been stunned by the sheer build up of comic book mailers one accumulates over time.

Unfortunately, this in itself is obviously unavoidable. I mean, realistically we, as much as yourselves, want that precious book that you've just purchased to arrive safe and sound, in an entirely mint condition. Right? Right.

BUT what is avoidable, is the continued production and purchase of pointlessly new and shiny mailers, each and every time, not to mention the felling of trees, and the deforestation of our rainforests due to the sheer waste generated by unecessary packaging. What's wrong with the ones you've got now? Be they covered in tape, or fresh as a dasiy, realistically there's not a single thing wrong with them. They function just as any other mailer should function, the same as a brand new one straight out the packet, and realistically, who even cares what the container looks like, as long as its doing its job and protecting its contents?

Therefore, what we offer at the Fantasy Road is an exclusive recycling program. The rules are simple:

  • For every 15 mailers you send back to us, we grant you £1 credit towards your next purchase.
  • If over 30 mailers are sent at one time, we will also refund you postage (up to £4.99), once recieved and the ammount has been confirmed.

That's it. Simple right? Sure, I hear you say "surely this is a cost saving method, using tatty mailers to lower expenditure", and yes - it is, but that's a passive effect of our true purpose, one that we hope will act as an incentive for comic book shops worldwide to participate in doing their part to make our world a better place.

Having said that, there are some rules and regulations that need to be followed.

  • All comic book mailers must be FIT FOR PURPOSE. This means that the cardboard mailer must not be torn, or damaged in a way that could risk the integrity of its contents.
  • All personal details (Name/address etc) must be removed from the mailer prior to sending to us. Unfortunately, that means that if personal details are written upon the mailer itself, we would not be able to accept it.
  • Comic book mailers DO NOT have to be originally from us. They can be from anywhere... Though please remove branding if present."
  • Comic book mailers that do not meet this criteria will be disposed of (in an enviromentally friendly fashion). These will not be mailed back, and will not count toward your recycling count, so please ensure that what you're sending follows the above guidelines.

So to finish things off. Sure, the mailer you recieve may not be the prettiest piece of cardboard that you've ever recieved, and sure, our branding may take a hit by our efforts to do something good. But what the hell. What would groot say? Well, yeah "I am groot", obviously. But you catch our drift.
