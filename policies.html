Policies

Privacy

Any information supplied to us is used only for shipping, and customer service response purposes. No information supplied is stored for any other purposes.

Sharing Informaion

Fantasy Road does not share your information with any third parties.

Shipping

All orders are sent tracked via Royal Mail, and are generally shipped once all items are in stock

Returns

Pre-owned books

Each display scan is an exact scan of the book you will be receiving, and are sold as shown. As such, we are unable to accept refunds on pre-owned book sales.

New books

Fantasy Road accepts returns of damaged comics, but we must be informed within 5 days of delivery. Please contact customerservice@fantasyroad.co.uk for any issues with orders or delivery.

Cancellations

Pre-orders

Due to the nature of collectables we do not accept cancellations on pre-orders.

In-stock items

Cancellations are accepted up to 24 hours of the order being placed, though due to charges applied on purchases (which we do not recieve back on cancellations), there will be an additional 4% admin charge.
