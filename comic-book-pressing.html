Comic Book Services

Fantasy Road is your one stop Comic Book shop - with services ranging from preowned sales, all the way to pressing, cleaning and CGC submission.

Comic Book Pressing & Cleaning

If you're looking to start your much-loved comic book's journey down the Fantasy Road toward CGC submission, pressing & cleaning is without a doubt the first stop. CGC graders use intricate specialised grading techniques to determine your book's final grade, these include things as obvious to tears and rips, all the way down to fingerprints and fibre-strain. Although pressing and cleaning will not repair the worst of damages, at Fantasy Road, using non-invasive techniques mastered over an age of practice - We can give your book the best possible chance possible to reach thier highest grade.

These services are also not limited just to raw copies - We also specialise in bumping grades of previously graded books. Send us your low grade slabs to be carefully broken open, cleaned, pressed, and if required, resubmitted for their new grade.
Please note: Grade bumps are not guaranteed - higher results are to be expected if the graded comic book has never been cleaned or pressed prior to grading.

As a final note, all comic books purchased on the Fantasy Road are eligible for pressing, cleaning, and CGC submission services. Please use the relevant button on checkout to select this service.

Submission

Fantasy Road can act as the middleman between yourselves and a number of CGC registered suppliers, such as Scott's Collectables. For CGC submission after pressing and cleaning, we charge a £3 handling fee for the first book, then an additional £1 per extra book on top of this, on top of any charges the registered CGC trader my require. This charge includes:

Middleman Service

  • Completion of the CGC Submission form to the relevant registered body.
  • Completion of submission requirements (E.G Scott's collectables require certain information to be written upon the accompanying backing board.)
  • Free backing board and protective comic bag (if not supplied) for submission, per book.
  • Re-packaging safely, with images provided upon completion.
  • Once per two weeks, we at Fantasy Road make the trip up to one of the authorised CGC traders - Therefore there is no charge for posting to the dealer. Whilst we provide this service, we can also appreciate that some people may not wish to wait for our delivery day, and therefore we also offer the option to send your books via an insured postal carrier of your choice. Proof of postage will be provided.

    Please note: Postage and insurance charges are additional to the handling fee. All items will be insured up to their full ammount, and must be paid in full before sending. We make no profit from this charge.

Any book also purchased at the Fantasy Road is eligible for CGC submission, please contact customersupport@fantasyroad.co.uk for more information.
